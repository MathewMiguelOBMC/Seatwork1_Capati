Favorite Foods

Favorite Foods from Filipino, American, and Italian

Crispy Pata

Crispy pata is a Filipino dish made from a deep-fried pork leg, typically simmered first until tender and then fried to a golden, crispy perfection. The result is a contrast of textures: a crunchy, crackling skin with juicy, flavorful meat inside.

Bistek Tagalog

Bistek, also known as Filipino beef steak, is a savory dish made with thinly sliced beef marinated in a mixture of soy sauce, calamansi (or lemon), garlic, and onions. The beef is pan-fried until tender and caramelized, then simmered with the marinade to create a flavorful, slightly tangy sauce.

Lumpia Shanghai

Lumpia Shanghai is a Filipino version of spring rolls, typically filled with a savory mixture of ground pork, shrimp, and vegetables, then rolled in a thin wrapper and deep-fried to golden, crispy perfection. The filling is seasoned with garlic, onion, soy sauce, and sometimes spices, creating a flavorful and savory bite.
